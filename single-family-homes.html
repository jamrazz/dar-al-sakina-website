Privacy. Warmth.A Place Called Home.

Each of our 20 single-family homes is designed from the ground up for Muslim seniors — balancing the serenity of private living with a genuine community just steps outside your door.

The Design Philosophy

Every Detail, Considered

Our homes were not adapted from generic senior housing templates. They were designed from a blank page with one question: what does a dignified, faith-rooted home truly need?


The result is a single-storey home that flows naturally — from a welcoming foyer to a sun-filled living room, a fully equipped kitchen, a dedicated prayer room, and a private garden opening to the shared courtyard. Nothing institutional. Entirely yours.

What’s Included

Built-In Home Features

🚪

Step-Free Entry

Zero-threshold entries at the front door and all room transitions. No steps, no barriers, complete ease of movement throughout.

🚿

Accessible Bathroom

Curbless shower with a fold-down bench and reinforced grab bars that blend beautifully with the decor — dignified by design.

🕌

Private Prayer Room

A dedicated musalla oriented to the qibla, with a built-in wudu area and soft natural light for peaceful daily worship.

🍳

Full Halal Kitchen

Generous counter space, dual-zone prep areas, and easy-reach storage — thoughtfully designed for seniors who love to cook.

🌳

Private Garden

Each home opens to an enclosed personal garden with raised planting beds, shaded seating, and a gate to the shared courtyard.

📶

Smart Home Ready

Pre-wired for smart lighting, emergency call systems, and keyless entry — keeping family connected with ease.
